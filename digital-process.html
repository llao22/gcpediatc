Digital Processes and Services

Modernize our service delivery and keep moving to digital.

Modernize and streamline our business processes by re-engineering them to be more efficient by:

  • Offering industry partners and the Canadian public better access via digital services.
  • Giving TC employees the necessary tools, information management and system infrastructure they need to enable their reporting and enhanced data-based decision-making.
  • Updating fees for current services and creating fees for new activities and for services that provide a commercial advantage to industry.
  • Building our capacity to certify aircraft, which will strengthen Canada's aerospace sector
  • Leading a review of aircraft services.
