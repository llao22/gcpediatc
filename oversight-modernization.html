Oversight Modernization

Modernize our oversight and regulatory regime.

Achieve a more streamlined, integrated and effective approach to the management, design, and delivery of regulatory and oversight programs. Success factors include:

  • Assessing relative safety and security risks across all modes to allow us to manage risks to the transportation system as a whole.
  • Gathering and better using data to improve our situational awareness so we can move more proactively on emerging issues.
  • Seeking opportunities to increase delegation to stakeholders, where risk and performance warrant.
  • Providing support to our inspectors in becoming more efficient by equipping them with mobile technology and tools to do their job the same way in the office and the field.
