Legislative Modernization

Modernize our safety and security legislative framework.

Laws that govern our transportation safety and security mandate must be updated to ensure they include a comprehensive range of clear authorities to:

  • Better achieve our safety and security mandate.
  • Allow us to anticipate, target and rapidly respond to the current and future demands of the transportation sector.
  • Updating fees for current services and creating fees for new activities and for services that provide a commercial advantage to industry.
  • Support evidence- and risk-based proactive decision-making, that enable timely response to risk.
